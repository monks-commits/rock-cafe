Dnipro Rock Cafe - a legendary venue that became the center of rock culture in the city for two decades. From 2001 to 2021, the cafe went through several locations, each leaving its unique mark on the musical history of Dnipro.

It was a place where musicians, rock music fans, creative people and those who sought the atmosphere of real rock gathered. Concerts, meetings, rehearsals and unforgettable evenings took place here.

Over 20 years of existence, Rock Cafe became not just an establishment, but a real cultural phenomenon, a symbol of the era and a place where legends were born.
