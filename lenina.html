The move to Lenina Street in 2011 marked a new stage in the development of Rock Cafe. It was a period of experiments and searching for new forms.

The new location was larger than the previous one, which allowed expanding the program of events. A full-fledged stage and improved sound system appeared here for the first time.

This period became a time of active development - the cafe began to attract attention not only of local musicians, but also artists from other cities.

However, after two years it became clear that the location's potential was exhausted, and it was decided to move again.

The interior of the "Lenina" location reflected the transitional period in the cafe's development. Elements of the old style were combined with new solutions here.

The main innovation was a full-fledged stage with professional lighting. The sound system was significantly improved for conducting larger-scale events.

The space was divided into several zones - concert, bar and recreation areas. This allowed conducting different types of events simultaneously.
