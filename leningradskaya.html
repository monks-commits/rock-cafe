The "Leningradskaya" location became the most successful and longest in the history of Rock Cafe. Five years at this venue became a real golden age of the establishment.

Here the cafe reached the peak of its popularity and became a recognized center of rock culture not only of Dnepropetrovsk, but of the entire region. The space was ideally adapted for conducting events of any scale.

This period is characterized by stability, audience growth and the appearance of regular programs. The cafe became a place where musicians from all over Ukraine aspired to get.

By 2018 it became clear that it was time for a new stage of development, and it was decided to move.

The interior of the "Leningradskaya" location represented an ideal combination of functionality and atmosphere. The space was designed specifically for musical events.
