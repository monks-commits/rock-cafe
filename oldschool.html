Old School Caf√© became an embodiment of nostalgia and return to the roots of rock culture. After the period of active development at "Leningradskaya" it was decided to create a more chamber and authentic space.

The cafe concept was based on "old school" principles - simplicity, authenticity and focus on music. There was no place for modern technologies and commercialization.

This period became a time of rethinking values and return to the roots of rock culture. The cafe became a place for true music connoisseurs.

However, after a year it became clear that it was time for the final stage of development, and it was decided to make the last move.

The interior of Old School Caf√© was maintained in retro and nostalgia style. Vintage elements and authentic details were used here, creating the atmosphere of past times.

The main feature was a minimalist stage without modern equipment. The sound was natural, without electronic effects and processing.

The furniture was selected in the style of the 70s-80s, and the walls were decorated with posters and photos from the golden age of rock music. Every detail was thought out to create a special atmosphere.
