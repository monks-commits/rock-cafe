–ö 2011 –≥–æ–¥—É "–ü–æ–±–µ–¥–∞" —Å—Ç–∞–ª–∞ —Å–ª–∏—à–∫–æ–º –º–∞–ª–∞ –¥–ª—è —Ä–∞—Å—Ç—É—â–µ–π –ø–æ–ø—É–ª—è—Ä–Ω–æ—Å—Ç–∏ –∫–∞—Ñ–µ, –∏ –±—ã–ª–æ –ø—Ä–∏–Ω—è—Ç–æ —Ä–µ—à–µ–Ω–∏–µ –æ –ø–µ—Ä–µ–µ–∑–¥–µ –Ω–∞ –Ω–æ–≤—É—é –ª–æ–∫–∞—Ü–∏—é.

The "Pobeda" location became the first and longest in the history of Dnipro Rock Cafe. Opening in 2001, it lasted for 10 years, becoming a real legend of the city's rock scene.

It was here that the atmosphere was born, which later became the hallmark of Rock Cafe. A small room with minimalist interior, where the main thing was not decoration, but music and people.

In the first years, the cafe worked as an ordinary establishment, but gradually began to turn into a center of rock culture. The first concerts of local bands, meetings of musicians and rock fans took place here.

By 2011, "Pobeda" became too small for the growing popularity of the cafe, and it was decided to move to a new location.

The interior of the first location was maintained in the style of minimalism and functionality. The main principle was creating atmosphere, not demonstrating luxury.

The walls were decorated with concert posters, photos of musicians and graffiti. The furniture was simple and practical - wooden tables and chairs, comfortable for long evenings.

Special attention was paid to sound - the acoustic system was tuned specifically for live performances. A small stage allowed for chamber concerts.

Over 10 years of existence of the "Pobeda" location, many concerts of local and visiting bands took place here. It was a time of formation of the rock scene of Dnepropetrovsk.

The first concerts took place in a chamber setting, but gradually popularity grew, and the cafe became a known place for musicians from the entire region.

Posters of those years became real artifacts of the era - they reflected the spirit of the time and the style of rock culture of the early 2000s.

Many of those who started their journey in "Pobeda" later became famous musicians and made a significant contribution to the development of rock culture.
